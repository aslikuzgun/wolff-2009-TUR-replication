# wolff-2009-TUR-replication
# Project Description
This study replicates Wolff et al. (2009) with Turkish data, using the experimental design of Kim & Marsden (2026).

OSF project page: https://osf.io/cm3b9/overview

## References
Kim, J., & Marsden, H. (2026). Cross-linguistic constraints on subjecthood in causative psych verbs: An experimental investigation of Korean, Mandarin Chinese and English. Glossa Psycholinguistics, 5(1).

Wolff, P., Jeon, G. H., & Li, Y. (2009). Causers in English, Korean, and Chinese and the individuation of events. Language and Cognition, 1(2), 167-196.
